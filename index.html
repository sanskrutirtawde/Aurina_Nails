Frequently Asked Questions

How long do press-on nails last?
With proper application and care, our press-on nails can last 1-2 weeks per wear. Each set is reusable 5-7 times, making them a cost-effective choice for beautiful nails.
How do I apply press-on nails?
We provide detailed instructions with each order. The basic process: clean your nails, apply the included adhesive tabs or glue, press firmly for 30 seconds. Full video tutorial available on our Instagram!
What's your turnaround time for custom orders?
Basic designs: 2-3 days. Premium/complex designs: 3-5 days. Rush orders available for an additional fee. We'll always communicate estimated delivery when you place your order.
Do you ship outside Pune?
Yes! We ship across India. Shipping costs vary by location. Contact us on WhatsApp with your pincode for accurate shipping estimates.
Can I request a specific design?
Absolutely! We love custom requests. Send us inspiration photos on WhatsApp or Instagram, and we'll create a design just for you. Custom design pricing starts at ‚Çπ599.
How do I know my nail size?
We'll send you a sizing guide when you place your order. You can measure your nails with a ruler or use our printable sizing chart. We create each set to your exact measurements for a perfect fit.
What's the difference between press-ons and extensions?
Press-ons are temporary, reusable, and applied at home. Extensions are semi-permanent, applied professionally, and last 2-3 weeks. Press-ons don't damage your natural nails and offer more flexibility to change styles.
